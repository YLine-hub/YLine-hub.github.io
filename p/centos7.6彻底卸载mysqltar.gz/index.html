1

  • 查看mysql状态
1

  • 关闭mysql
1

/etc/init.d/mysqld stop

查找mysql的安装目录并彻底删除

  • 查找mysql有关的目录
1
2

whereis mysql
find / -name mysql

  • 全部删除
1
2
3
4

rm -rf /usr/lib64/mysql/
rm -rf /usr/local/mysql/
rm -rf /run/sudo/ts/mysql
rm -rf /etc/selinux/targeted/active/modules/100/mysql

  • /etc/my.cnf
  • /etc/init.d/mysqld
